3 W’S YOU SHOULD KNOW ABOUT NOC

  • WHAT IS NOC ?

    NOC stands for No Objection Certificate. It is a legal document which is issued from the regional RTO in case of re-registration of a vehicle from one state or union territory to any other or even inter-state. This is as per regulations that you are required to apply for a change of address and re-register when you are going to another state for over a year. The major reason behind this is for keeping track of vehicles and for quick actions in case of any situation in a particular territory. Updating the records will ease the process mainly for security purposes.

  • WHO NEEDS IT ?

    Change of place of residence . New residence is in the jurisdiction of some other RTO Selling the vehicle to a person who resides in some other RTO area.

    The original purpose for applying a new NOC is to permit a change of the local government authority jurisdiction on that vehicle from one state to the other state’s or inter-state Thelying a new NOC jurisdiction.
    A vehicle owner, who is about to change its RTO from one state to another permanently, should apply for it.

  • WHY YOU NEED IT ?

    The original purpose for applying a new NOC is to permit a change of the local government authority jurisdiction on that vehicle from one state to the other state’s or inter-state jurisdiction. In simple words, if you shifting your vehicle because of either change of residence or you are selling to someone who lives in other RTO jurisdiction , then there will be change of address/ change of ownership. This NOC or no objection certificate is issued by parent RTO certifying that there is no traffic chalan, crime or dues is pending against the vehicle.

HOW TO APPLY FOR NOC OF VEHICLE

  1. 1. Download , fill it up and get signature of the registered owner on the form.
  2. 2. Get pencil impression of chassis no. on the
  3. 3. Arrange NOC letter from bank if vehicle in on loan or under hypothecation
  4. 4. Arrange valid supporting documents required to get NOC for vehicle Submit the application for NOC along with supporting documents to the RTO where vehicle registered
  5. 5. Wait for processing of NOC application at RTO , it takes approx 1 month time
  6. 6. Collect physical copy of NOC issued from RTO

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED FOR NOC

  1. 1. 4 copies of Application on Form 28 along with pencil impression of chachis no.
  2. 2. Original RC/ Copy of RC
  3. 3. Valid pollution clearance certificate Copy of valid insurance
  4. 4. Upto date payment of Road tax
  5. 5. Crime Report of N.C.R.B.
  6. 6. Copy of address proof of the registered owner or the buyer where the vehicle is moving
